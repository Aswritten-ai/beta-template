*   **What gets saved as memories?** Strategic pivots, positioning decisions, technical ADRs, and proof pointsâ€”anything that defines the organization's worldview.
*   **What's the review process?** Memories are committed to Git, allowing for a standard PR-style review to ensure accuracy before they update the "Foundation."

*   **What happens when someone leaves?** Their "witnessed" contributions remain in the graph, ensuring zero institutional knowledge loss.
